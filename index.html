Our Mission

We provide help and inspire hope to those affected by breast cancer through early detection, education, and support services. SheFoundation aims to connect people of all ages, races, ethnicities, genders, sexual identities, and locations to researchers committed to solving important breast cancer questions. Our goal is to accelerate and increase diversity in breast cancer research, and forge partnerships between our members and the scientific community.

Supporting Breast Cancer Patients

We offer Programs and Services to help you during and after cancer treatment. We can also help you find other free or low-cost resources available. Our 24/7 cancer helpline provides support for people dealing with cancer. We can connect you with trained cancer information specialists who will answer questions about cancer diagnosis and provide guidance and a compassionate ear.
